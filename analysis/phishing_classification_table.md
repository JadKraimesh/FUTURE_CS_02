## Summary

The analyzed phishing samples demonstrate common social engineering techniques used by attackers to trick users into revealing sensitive information.

Common indicators identified include:

- Spoofed sender domains
- Fake login links
- Urgent or threatening language
- Impersonation of trusted brands
- Requests for immediate action

Users should always verify email legitimacy before clicking links or providing credentials.
