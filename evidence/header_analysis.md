# Email Header Analysis

## Tools Used

- Google Admin Toolbox Messageheader
- MXToolbox Header Analyzer

---

## Safe Email Header Findings

### Result
- Sender domain aligned with official LinkedIn domain
- No spoofing indicators identified
- SPF/DKIM alignment appeared legitimate

### Conclusion
The email header appeared authentic and trustworthy.

---

## Suspicious Email Header Findings

### Result
- Sender domain mismatched expected PayPal domain
- Suspicious reply path detected
- Domain reputation questionable

### Conclusion
The email demonstrated potential spoofing behavior and impersonation indicators.

---

## Phishing Email Header Findings

### Result
- Fake Microsoft-related sender domain
- Untrusted mail routing behavior
- Header inconsistencies identified
- Potential spoofing indicators detected

### Conclusion
The email strongly resembled credential phishing activity.
